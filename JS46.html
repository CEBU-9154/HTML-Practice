Forsaken (fan-made) Wiki!

Welcome to The Fan-Made Forsaken Wiki! This Wiki holds many information from characters to lore and more based off the Roblox Game FORSAKEN. Forsaken is an 8v1 asymmetrical horror game that follows 10 Survivors trying to survive on 1 of 10 maps from 1 of the 4 possible killers under a time limit that increases from every kill.


For those who are new to this game, Forsaken is split into two classes: Survivors and Killers. Check out their pages to learn more!
