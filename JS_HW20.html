Pizza:

A dish consisting of a round, flattened base of leavened dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, pepperonies, and so much more.)




Pastries:

Pastry refers to the sweet and savory baked goods. These goods are often called pastries as a synecdoche and sweetened pastries are often describes as bakers' confectionery.
